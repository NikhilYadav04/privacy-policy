Privacy Policy for PillBin

Introduction

PillBin is a drug disposal and health-support application designed to help users responsibly manage medications and access useful tools such as medicine tracking, disposal guidance, and health report analysis. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our app.

Information We Collect

To provide our services, PillBin may collect certain user information through forms within the app. This may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

All information is provided voluntarily by the user and is stored securely using industry-standard practices.

How Your Data Is Used

Your information is used only to provide and improve app features, including:

  • User authentication and account management
  • Sending verification messages (such as email-based OTPs)
  • Providing health-related tools and medicine management features
  • Improving the overall user experience

PillBin does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Location Information

PillBin may request access to your device’s location to enable location-based features within the app.

  • Location data is used only to help users find nearby medicine disposal centers.
  • The app displays disposal centers based on the user’s current location and selected radius (for example, 5 km or 10 km).
  • Location access is used only when the user explicitly chooses to search for nearby disposal centers.
  • PillBin does not continuously track your location.
  • Location data is not stored permanently and is processed temporarily to provide search results.

Location information is not shared with third parties and is used strictly to improve the accuracy of nearby disposal center recommendations.

PDF Scanning Feature

PillBin includes a PDF scanning and health report analysis feature. Please note:

  • Uploaded PDF files are stored temporarily on secure servers.
  • PillBin does not permanently store PDF documents or extracted health data.
  • Uploaded files are automatically deleted when the session ends or the user logs out.
  • No scanned files or extracted data are shared with third parties.

Data Security

We take user privacy seriously and apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption, restricted access, and regular security monitoring.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Third-Party Links

PillBin may include links to external websites or resources for educational or informational purposes. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of third-party websites, and users are encouraged to review their privacy policies independently.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in app features, legal requirements, or operational practices. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically.
